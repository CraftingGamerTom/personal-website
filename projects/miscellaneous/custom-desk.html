Custom Desk

Description

I had a very small desk for many years. I had a 4 foot long, 20 inches wide desk that was the home for a keyboard, mouse, and two 32 inch monitors. I also had a need for a place to be able to write. I was completing homework assinments in my lap. I needed a bigger desk.

Solution

I created a simple 6ft by 6ft L-Shaped desk with a 3/4 inch plywood top that was sanded to a very fine touch. I attached the top with angle brackets and I cut holes for wire management purposes.

The desk is constructed of two parts that are held together with nuts bolts underneath. One part is 6ft by 6ft, the other is 6ft by 4ft. It is heavy but structurally sound. The ability to take the desk apart was key to its portability. I wanted to easily be able to take the desk wherever I lived.
