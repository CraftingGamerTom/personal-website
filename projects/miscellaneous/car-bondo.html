Car Bondo Project

Overview

My 2000 Toyota Corolla was plagued with the cancer, that is rust. I spent some time, with the help of my former High School, fixing it.

I grinded down the rust and metal, replaced needed areas with metal mesh, used Bondo on the holes, primed the metal, painted (matching my care color), and added a clear coat. There was many stages of sanding. Not only the rust, but also the prime layer, and even paint. It was a great learning experience and the job stood the test of time.
