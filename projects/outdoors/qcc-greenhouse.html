QCC Greenhouse Volunteering

Plant Watering

Though I have worked with the greenhouse for a long time. I am focusing this post on the semester I took on the responsibility of weekend plant watering.

During this semester I was taking classes 6 days per week. Monday-Saturday. I was the only involved student who was on campus regularly on Saturdays so I happily took on the job. I had class from 12pm-4pm. I would water some plants before class and others after class, depending on the weather and level of sun.

This time was very relaxing for me. I was alone, it was quiet. Just the soft hum of a local radio station our advisor leaves on for the plants to have something to listen to. This was a nice time for me to relax. The semester flew by. It never really felt like work. Well, accept those hard to reach plants, but they needed water too. I loved caring for them. They are important to us all, at QCC. I was happy to help by watering, though most of my contributions are through the greenhouse software (web application).
