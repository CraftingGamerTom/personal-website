Flower bed Overhaul

Overview

A family friend had many over grown flower beds. I spent a bit if my summer cleaning them up and making new flower beds. Many of the existing flower beds were overgrown with weeds. He asked me to dig all the roots out of the weeds and remove any dirt with roots to ensure they were removed. I moved hundreds of wheel barrows filled with dirt. I created a very impressive platform of removed dirt in the woods. Demensions estimated at 20x15x3 ft and another 10x10x3. Both were well packed down. It was an amazing accomplishment to see so much honest hard work pay off when the flowers we planted.

The next summer we replaced some dirt. The owner felt the beds were too low, I agreed. Mulching and replanting flowers was in order again. I was granted the task to also dig some holes for trees.

I am always happy to get my hands dirty. It was a wonderful opportunity.

If I ever see myself needing to complete a job like the one I took on, I think I will rent a Bobcat!
